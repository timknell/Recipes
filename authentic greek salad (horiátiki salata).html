Authentic Greek Salad Horiátiki Salata

Source: https://www.mediterraneanliving.com/authentic-greek-salad-horiatiki-salata/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 3 medium tomatoes (quartered)
  • 1 cucumber (peeled and sliced)
  • 1/4 red onion (cut into strips)
  • 1/4 red bell pepper (cut into thin strips)
  • 10 kalamata olives
  • 6 ounces feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 splash red wine vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp oregano

Preparation

1. Add all veggies and olives to a bowl.

2. Top with feta cheese, olive oil, lemon juice (or vinegar), and oregano. Salt and pepper, to taste.
