Moms Spanish Rice

Source: https://www.tasteofhome.com/recipes/mom-s-spanish-rice/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 4

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1 medium green pepper (chopped)
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (14-1/2 ounces) no-salt-added diced tomatoes (drained)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2-2/3 cups brown rice (cooked)

Preparation

1. In a large skillet, cook beef, onion and pepper over medium heat 6-8 minutes or until beef is no longer pink and onion is tender, breaking up beef into crumbles; drain.

2. Stir in tomato sauce, tomatoes and seasonings; bring to a boil. Add rice; heat through, stirring occasionally.
