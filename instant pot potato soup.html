Instant Pot Potato Soup

Source: https://www.pressurecookrecipes.com/instant-pot-potato-soup/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 6

Description

Make Easy Creamy Instant Pot Potato Soup with a few simple ingredients! Deliciously umami pressure cooker potato soup is like a comforting hug that warms your heart.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pound (680 g) Yukon gold potatoes , chunked
  • 1/2 pound (226 g) small red potatoes , chunked
  • 4 cups (1 L) unsalted chicken stock
  • 6 cloves (30 g) garlic (minced)
  • 6 slices (215 g) bacon (chopped)
  • 1 medium onion (diced)
  • 1 cup (250 ml) heavy cream
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 stalks green onions (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons (30 ml) fish sauce

Preparation

1. Crisp Bacon: Crisp 6 slices of bacon or chopped bacon in Instant Pot - add bacon and heat up Instant Pot using SautÃ© More function. Stir occasionally and allow the bacon to crisp. Remove bacon from Instant Pot and place them on a paper towel to absorb the excess fat. *Pro Tip: You don't need to add oil as bacon has a lot of fat.

2. Saute Onions and Garlic: Add diced onion in the bacon fat. Saute until soften (~2 mins). Add in minced garlic, 2 bay leaves, then saute for another minute.

3. Deglaze: Pour Â½ cup (125ml) unsalted chicken stock. Scrub all the brown bits off the bottom of the pot with a wooden spoon.  *Pro Tip: Keep the brown bits as they are very flavorful.

4. Pressure Cook Potato Soup: Add in potato chunks, 2 tbsp (30ml) fish sauce, and 3Â½ cups (875ml) unsalted chicken stock. Give it a quick mix. Pressure cook at High Pressure for 3 minutes + 15 minutes Natural Release.

5. Blend & Season: Discard bay leaves. Blend potato soup into desired consistency with an immersion hand blender. Add 1 cup (250ml) heavy cream. Taste the potato soup and season with more salt. For reference, we added 3 large pinches of kosher salt.
