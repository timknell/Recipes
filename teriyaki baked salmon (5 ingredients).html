Teriyaki Baked Salmon 5 Ingredients

Source: Lose It blog

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb salmon filets
  • 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  • 2 medium heads broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 400F, line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Place broccoli florets on prepared baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil. Salt & pepper the broccoli, roast for 10 minutes.
3. While broccoli cooks, pat salmon dry and place skin-down in a small baking dish. Pour the teriyaki sauce over the salmon, coat the top and sides.
4. After broccoli has been cooking for 10 minutes, add the salmon to the oven. Cook both for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through. Recommended internal temperature: 145F
5.Plate salmon and broccoli, squeeze lime on top and drizzle with any remaining teriyaki sauce.
