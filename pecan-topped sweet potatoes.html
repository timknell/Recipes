PecanTopped Sweet Potatoes

Rating: 0.0

Description

Serve these yummy sweet potatoes at your next Thanksgiving feast and be ready for lots of compliments. This doubles well for a crowd. Diane Romney

Ingredients

  • 3 cups sweet potatoes (cooked and mashed)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup Butter
  • 2 eggs (well beaten)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons Butter (melted)
  • 1/2 cup pecan pieces

Preparation

Preheat oven to 350°. In a large bowl mix the first six ingredients and place into a 2 quart casserole. Combine last four ingredients and sprinkle on top of sweet potatoes. Bake uncovered at 350° for 25 to 30 minutes, or until top is crisp.
