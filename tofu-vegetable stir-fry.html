TofuVegetable StirFry

Source: http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/food-network-kitchen/tofu-vegetable-stir-fry-recipe-2118824

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice ((jasmine rice preferred))
  • 3 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Asian chile sauce
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 4 scallions (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 1-inch piece ginger (peeled and finely chopped)
  • 4 ounces shiitake mushrooms (stemmed and chopped)
  • 4 ounces snow peas
  • 1 14-ounce package soft tofu (drained and cut into 1-inch cubes)

Preparation

1. Cook the rice as the label directs. Meanwhile, whisk the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, vinegar, chile sauce, cornstarch and 1 cup water in a small bowl until smooth; set aside.

2. Heat the sesame oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the scallion whites, garlic and ginger and stir-fry 30 seconds. Add the mushrooms and stir-fry until golden brown and tender, about 3 minutes. Add the snow peas and stir-fry 30 more seconds. Whisk the reserved soy sauce mixture and add it to the wok. Bring to a simmer, then add the tofu. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the sauce is thick, about 2 minutes. Sprinkle with the scallion greens. Fluff the rice with a fork and divide among bowls.
