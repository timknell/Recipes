Original New York Cheesecake

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter (melted)
  • 2 1/2 pounds cream cheese (in room temperature)
  • 1/8 teaspoon table salt
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 can cherry pie filing

Preparation

Prepare the Crust

1. Heat oven to 325 degrees. Mix graham cracker crumbs and sugar in medium bowl with 5 tablespoons melted butter.

2. Brush bottom and sides of a 9-inch springform pan with 1/2 tablespoon melted butter. Pour crumbs into springform pan and press evenly into pan bottom. Bake until fragrant and beginning to brown around edges, about 13 minutes. Remove and cool while making filling.


Prepare Filling

3. Heat oven to 500 degrees. Using a mixer, beat cream cheese at medium-low speed to break up and soften slightly, about 1 minute.

4. Add salt and about half of sugar and beat at medium-low speed for about 1 minute. Add the remaining sugar until combined, for another 1 minute.

5. Scrape the sides and bottom of the bowl. Add sour cream, lemon juice, and vanilla, and beat at low speed for another minute. Add egg yolks and beat at medium-low speed until thoroughly combined.

6. Scrape bowl and add whole eggs two at a time, beating until thoroughly combined, about 1 minute, and scraping bowl between additions.

7. Brush sides of springform pan with remaining 1/2 tablespoon melted butter. Pour filling into cooled crust and bake 10 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 200 degrees and continue to bake for about 1 1/2 hours.

8. Remove cake from the oven and cool for 3 hours. Cover and refrigerate until cold for at least  3 hours.

9. Top with cherry pie filling before serving.
