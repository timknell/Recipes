Recipe Sheet Pan Italian Sausage and Veggies

Source: https://www.thekitchn.com/italian-sausage-vegetables-268349

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 4

Description

If using Aidell’s sausages, cook for less than the full 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound small red potatoes (halved)
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 (12-ounce) package smoked Italian-style chicken sausage (cut into 1-inch pieces)
  • 1 medium red bell pepper (cored, seeded, and chopped)
  • 1 small head broccoli (cut into bite-size florets)

Preparation

1. Arrange a rack in the middle of the oven and heat to 400ºF. Place a rimmed baking sheet in the oven while it is heating.

2. Place the potatoes, salt, and 1 tablespoon of the oil in a medium bowl and toss to combine. Transfer to the baking sheet, add the sausages, and spread into an even layer.

3. Roast for 15 minutes. Meanwhile, place the pepper, broccoli, and remaining 1 tablespoon of oil in the now-empty bowl and toss to coat.

4. Stir the sausage and potatoes and push to one side of the baking sheet. Add the pepper and broccoli on the other side and spread into an even layer. Roast until the sausage is browned and the vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes more.
