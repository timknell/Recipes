Chicken Tostadas with Avocado Sauce

Source: https://www.goodhousekeeping.com/food-recipes/a10293/chicken-tostadas-avocado-sauce-recipe-ghk0810/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves (packed)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 c reduced-fat sour cream
  • 1/4 c fresh lime juice
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 orange bell pepper
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 3 cups coarsely shredded chicken meat
  • 4 plum tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup crumbled fresh Mexican cheese (such as queso fresco)

Preparation

1. Prepare avocado sauce: In blender, puree avocado, cilantro, garlic, sour cream, lime juice, water, and 1/2 teaspoon salt until very smooth. If making ahead, cover and refrigerate up to 1 day.

2. In large bowl, combine lettuce, onion, and pepper. Cover and refrigerate vegetables up to 1 day.

3. To serve, place 1 corn tortilla between 2 paper towels. Microwave on High 1 minute to 1 minute 15 seconds or until tortilla is golden and crisp. Repeat with remaining tortillas. Place 1 tortilla on each of 4 serving plates.

4. In medium bowl, toss shredded chicken with 3/4 cup avocado sauce. In bowl with lettuce mixture, toss 1/8 teaspoon salt and remaining sauce.

5. Divide chicken among tortillas. Top with lettuce mixture, then tomato slices. Sprinkle cheese over tomatoes. Garnish with lime wedges.
