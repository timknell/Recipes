Creamy Tomato Soup

Source: http://www.myrecipes.com/recipe/creamy-tomato-soup-50400000126834/

Rating: 5.0

Servings: 6

Description

Smoked paprika and cumin round out the sweetness of San Marzano tomatoes. If you can't find these tomatoes, use a can of regular unsalted tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 3/8 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon hot smoked paprika
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 (14-ounce) can San Marzano tomatoes (chopped unsalted) (undrained)
  • 2 cups unsalted chicken stock (such as Swanson)
  • 1/4 cup half-and-half
  • 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

1. Heat a medium saucepan over medium heat. Add canola oil to pan; swirl to coat. Add onion, cumin, kosher salt, and paprika to pan; cook 8 minutes, stirring occasionally. Increase the heat to medium-high. Add minced garlic; saut√© 1 minute, stirring constantly. Stir in tomatoes and chicken stock; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat; stir in half-and-half.

2. Place half of tomato mixture in a blender. Remove center piece of blender lid (to allow steam to escape); secure blender lid on blender. Place a clean towel over opening in blender lid (to avoid splatters). Blend until smooth. Pour into a large bowl. Repeat procedure with remaining tomato mixture. Top with parsley and freshly ground black pepper.
