Crock Pot Ginger Ale Chicken Taco Salad With Cilantro Vinaigrette

Source: https://sweetpeasandsaffron.com/slow-cooker-beer-chicken-taco-salad-with-cilantro-vinaigrette/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 4

Description

meal prep recipes for busy people

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 6 oz ginger ale
  • 6 oz chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 can green chiles 114 mL/3.8 oz
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup cilantro leaves loosely packed
  • 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice
  • 3 tablespoons honey 2 tablespoons of maple syrup makes a nice substitute
  • 1/2 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 handful of nacho chips
  • 1/2 cup cucumber cubed
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes halved
  • 1/2 bell pepper cut into small pieces
  • 1 tablespoon red onion diced
  • jalapeno slices optional

Preparation

1. Place all ingredients from chicken to black pepper in the base of a slow cooker.

2. Cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours.

3. Transfer the chicken to a bowl and shred with two forks.

4. Toss with cooking liquid, to taste. I used about Â¾ of the cooking liquid.

5. Cilantro Vinaigrette: Combine all ingredients from cilantro to salt in a blender, food processor, or cup of an immersion blender.

6. Blend until smooth.

7. Toss the vegetables and chicken with the cilantro vinaigrette.

8. Serve with nachos, sour cream, guacamole and/or salsa.
