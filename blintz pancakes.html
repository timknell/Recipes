Blintz Pancakes

Source: https://www.tasteofhome.com/recipes/blintz-pancakes/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 12

Description

Blending sour cream and cottage cheese, ingredients traditionally associated with blintzes, into the batter of these pancakes gives them old-fashioned flavor. Top these family favorites with berry syrup to turn an ordinary morning into an extraordinary day.â€”Dianna Digoy, San Diego, California

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup Daisy 4% cottage cheese
  • 4 large eggs (lightly beaten)

Preparation

1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar and salt. Stir in the sour cream, cottage cheese and eggs until blended.

2. Pour batter by 1/4 cupfuls onto a greased hot griddle in batches; turn when bubbles form on top. Cook until the second side is golden brown. Serve with syrup and, if desired, strawberries.

Cook thoroughly at 325
