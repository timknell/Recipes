Roasted Curried Cauliflower Soup

Source: Blendtec recipe book

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 5

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower (chopped into florets, approx. 6 cups)
  • 1 onion (sliced into 1/4” rings)
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 Tbsp curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 3 cups water (warmed)
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable broth powder

Preparation

Preheat oven to 425.
Toss the first five ingredients and spread onto a baking sheet or pan.
Roast vegetables for 25 minutes.
Add water and vegetable broth powder to jar with 3 cups of roasted cauliflower and onions, secure lid.
Press “Speed Up” to speed 6.
Pour 1 cup of soup into each bowl and top with approximately 1/2 cup of roasted vegetables.
