Fettuccine with Lemon Ricotta and Zucchini Recipe

Source: https://www.savorynothings.com/fettuccine-with-lemon-ricotta-and-zucchini/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 4

Description

Fettuccine with Lemon Ricotta and Zucchini Recipe - A fresh and tasty pasta dish for summer, from kitchen to table in no time!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Fettuccine 340 g
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large zucchini halved lengthwise and cut into ribbons
  • 1 large yellow summer squash halved lengthwise and cut into ribbons
  • 2 medium carrots cut into ribbons
  • 1 cup Ricotta cheese 250 g

Preparation

1. Cook pasta according to package directions.

2. Heat the oil in a large skillet and cook the vegetables together with the lemon peel over medium-high heat soft and slightly colored.

3. To make the ricotta sauce, stir all ingredients together in a bowl and season with salt and pepper to taste.

4. Drain the pasta, reserving 1/4 cup of cooking liquid. Add the pasta back to the pot with the ricotta sauce and the reserved liquid. Carefully mix in the vegetables and serve immediately.
