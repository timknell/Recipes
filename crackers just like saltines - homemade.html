Crackers Just Like Saltines Homemade

Source: http://www.food.com/recipe/crackers-just-like-saltines-homemade-56886

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 20

Ingredients

  • 4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup shortening
  • 1 1/3 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Preparation

1. Mix flour, baking powder,& shortening and then add milk; blend well.

2. Form a ball with the dough and divide into 4 sections.

3. Roll 1 section out very thin (about 1/8 to 3/16-inches) -- making sure it is rolled out EVENLY -- and cut with a cookie cutter (you can use all shapes!) or a pastry wheel with the crinkled edges, and repeat with other sections.

4. place on an ungreased cookie sheet& prick the dough with a fork several times and sprinkle with salt.

5. Bake at 375 for 12-20 minutes until golden brown, but be careful not to burn!
