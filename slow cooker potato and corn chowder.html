Slow Cooker Potato and Corn Chowder

Source: http://damndelicious.net/2014/09/12/slow-cooker-potato-corn-chowder/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 8

Description

The easiest chowder you will ever make. Throw everything in the crockpot and you're set! Easy peasy!

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces red potato (diced)
  • 1 (16-ounce) package frozen corn
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 6 cups chicken stock
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup instant potatoes (optional)

Preparation

1. Place potatoes and corn into a slow cooker. Stir in flour and gently toss to combine. Stir in chicken stock, thyme, oregano, garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper, to taste.

2. Cover and cook on low heat for 7-8 hours or high heat for 3-4 hours.* Stir in butter and heavy cream.

3. Serve immediately.

(Soup will be on the thin side...can add instant potato flakes to thicken)
