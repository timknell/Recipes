Tropical Chicken Lettuce Wraps Recipe

Source: https://www.southernliving.com/recipes/tropical-chicken-lettuce-wraps-recipe

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 6

Description

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rotisserie chicken (shredded)
  • 2 tablespoons green onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 tablespoon jalapeño pepper (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon lime zest plus 1 tbsp fresh juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 12 leaves butter lettuce
  • 1 cup 1 /2-inch cubed mango
  • 1/2 cup coarsely chopped cashews (roasted, lightly salted)
  • 1/4 cup packed fresh cilantro leaves

Preparation

1. Toss together shredded chicken and green onions in a medium bowl. Whisk together mayonnaise, honey, jalapeño, lime zest, lime juice, salt, and pepper in a small bowl. Gently stir mayonnaise mixture into chicken mixture until combined.

2. Divide chicken mixture evenly among lettuce leaves (about 1⁄4 cup per leaf), and top evenly with mango, cashews, and cilantro leaves. Serve with lime wedges.
