Scrambled Eggs in a Mug

Source: https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/257530/scrambled-eggs-in-a-mug/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 1

Description

These delicious scrambled eggs are quick and easy to make, and for all the family.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon milk

Preparation

1. Crack egg into a bowl; beat until even in color. Pour in milk and beat until light yellow in color. Salt and pepper. 

2. Spray mug or ramekin with cooking spray, pour egg in. 

3. Heat in microwave until cooked through and fluffy. Start with 30 seconds, remove from microwave and mix. Microwave 15 seconds and mix again. Continue in 15 or 10 second intervals until done.
