Ginny Special

Source: Geneva Knell

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • not too much mustard
  • 2 slices Bread, whole wheat
  • 2 slices white american cheese
  • ? mayonnaise

Preparation

Get out bread and put mustard on one slice and mayo on the other. Then put on cheese. After that, put mayo on the mustard side and mustard on the mayo side. Put the bread together and you are finished!
