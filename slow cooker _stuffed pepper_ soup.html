Slow Cooker Stuffed Pepper Soup

Rating: 0.0

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey or beef
  • 1 cup onions (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic (minced)
  • 1 28-ounce can diced tomatoes (with juice)
  • 1 6-oz can tomato paste
  • 4 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 3 cups green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2/3 cup rice (uncooked)
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon (ground)

Preparation

1. In a large skillet, cook turkey, onion and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain any juices and transfer mixture to a slow cooker. 

2. Add remaining ingredients and stir gently to mix. Cook on low for 8 to 9 hours, adding more water if necessary.
