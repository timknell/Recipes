Crispy Fried Chicken

Rating: 5.0

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chicken pieces (patted dry)
  • 1/2 cup kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3 cups buttermilk
  • 2 onions (sliced thin)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cracked peppercorn
  • 2 cups vegetable shortening
  • 1 stick unsalted butter

Preparation

1. Put chicken pieces in a bowl and coat with kosher salt. Cover and refrigerate for an hour.

2. Rinse chicken well and put in bowl with buttermilk and onions. Cover and refrigerate overnight.

3. Put flour, cayenne, cracked pepper and 1 1/2 teaspoons salt in a large Ziploc bag and shake to combine well.

4. Pick up a piece of chicken and let the excess buttermilk drip off, put in bag and shake well.

5. Repeat with each piece until all pieces are coated well ‚Äì place them on waxed paper and leave out for 30 minutes to dry and come closer to room temperature.

6. Melt shortening and butter in a heavy skillet over high heat until hot but not smoking. Add chicken, cover pan and lower heat ‚Äì cook 10 minutes.

7. Turn chicken pieces and cook, uncovered until done (about 15 minutes) ‚Äì test by piercing each piece; juices must run clear.
