Pork Sweet and Sour

Rating: 0.0

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds pork (cooked)
  • 3/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 3 green peppers (cut in narrow strips)
  • 1 onions (cut in narrow strips)
  • 1 can pineapple chunks
  • 3 tablespoons corn starch
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons vinegar, white
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 cup brown rice

Preparation

Trim the pork of excess fat and slice. Marinade the pork in the 3/4 cup soy sauce. Let stand for one hour, turning occasionally. Heat the oil in a saut√© pan over high heat, cook peppers and onion for just one minute, stirring constantly. Remove from the pan and set aside. Drain the pork, add to the skillet along with the juice from the pineapple tidbits, and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and simmer for five minutes. Add the pineapple, peppers, and onion, stir to mix and simmer one minute. Combined the sauce ingredients thoroughly and add to the pork mixture, stirring constantly. Cook until sauce thickens.

Serve over rice.

If making with tofu, try adding 2 Tbsp more of soy sauce.
