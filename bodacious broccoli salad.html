Bodacious Broccoli Salad

Source: http://allrecipes.com/Recipe/Bodacious-Broccoli-Salad/Detail.aspx?evt19=1

Rating: 5.0

Servings: 12

Description

"This recipe is requested at every family gathering. Let it be your next dish that they crave! I like this dish to be prepared at least two hours before serving. Be sure to have copies of the recipe on hand, as everyone will ask for it!"" - Author

Ingredients

  • 8 slices bacon
  • 2 heads fresh broccoli (chopped)
  • 1 1/2 cups sharp Cheddar cheese (shredded)
  • 1/2 red onion (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/8 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice

Preparation

1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, and crumble.

2. In a large bowl, combine broccoli, cheese, bacon and onion.

3. Prepare the dressing in a small bowl by whisking together the red wine vinegar, sugar, pepper, salt, mayonnaise and lemon juice. Combine dressing with salad. Cover, and refrigerate until ready to serve.
