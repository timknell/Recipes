Baked Ziti I

Source: http://allrecipes.com/recipe/baked-ziti-i/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 10

Description

"A lady I worked with brought this in one day, and it was a hit. Now it is the favorite of all my dinner guests. It's great for a covered dish dinner too. I have made this also without the meat, and it is well received."" - Author

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onions (chopped)
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese (sliced)
  • 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese (grated)

Preparation

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ziti pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.

2. In a large skillet, brown onion and ground beef over medium heat. Add spaghetti sauce, and simmer 15 minutes.

3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer as follows: 1/2 of the ziti, Provolone cheese, sour cream, 1/2 sauce mixture, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese and remaining sauce mixture. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.

4. Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheeses are melted.
