Tomato Walnut Tilapia

Source: https://www.tasteofhome.com/recipes/tomato-walnut-tilapia/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 4

Description

Tomato, bread crumbs and crunchy walnuts dress up tilapia fillets in this favorite recipe. I serve it with cooked julienne carrots and green beans. â€”Phyl Broich-Wessling, Garner, Iowa

Ingredients

  • 4 tilapia fillets
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 medium tomato (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup soft bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup walnuts (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons butter (melted)

Preparation

1. Sprinkle fillets with salt and pepper. In a large skillet coated with cooking spray, cook fillets in butter over medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes on each side or until lightly browned.

2. Transfer fish to a broiler pan or baking sheet; top with tomato.

3. Combine the topping ingredients; spoon over the tops.

4. Broil 3-4 in. from the heat for 2-3 minutes or until topping is lightly browned and fish flakes easily with a fork.
