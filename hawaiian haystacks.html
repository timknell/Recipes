Hawaiian Haystacks

Source: https://www.epicurious.com/recipes/member/views/no-cream-of-soup-hawaiian-haystacks-50129332

Rating: 0.0

Description

Toppings: (items with a star are our favorites) shredded cheddar cheese* pineapple tidbits* sliced olives* diced green onion* chopped celery* shredded coconut* slice almonds* diced fresh mushroom Chow mein noodles* diced tomatoes peas mandarin oranges raisins diced green pepper water chestnuts cashews soy sauce or anything else that you think would be good

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup rice
  • 2 cups cooked and cubed chicken
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 1/2 tsp garlic (finely minced)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 green onion (trimmed)
  • 1 stalk celery (halved)
  • 15 oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can water chestnuts

Preparation

1. Steam the rice.

2. In a large sauce pan, saute the onion in the butter until soft and translucent. Add the garlic and cook for about one minute, stirring, until fragrant. 

3. Sprinkle the flour over the onions and stir to combine. Cook over medium heat for one minute. 

4. Slowly whisk in the milk and chicken broth. Cook, stirring constantly with a whisk, and bring the sauce to a simmer over medium. Add the salt and pepper. 

5. Add the chicken and simmer, stirring the sauce frequently, until the sauce has thickened, about 5-8 minutes. 

6. Prepare the toppings.

7. Serve the sauce over steamed rice with desired toppings from the list below. Serves 4-6.
