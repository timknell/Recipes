Sweet Potato Lasagna

Source: http://fitmencook.com/budget-sweet-potato-lasagna-meal-prep/

Rating: 0.0

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pound lean ground turkey
  • 14 oz sweet potatoes (sliced thinly)
  • 1 egg whites
  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 15 oz tomato sauce
  • 1/3 cup mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup red onion
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 8 tbsp fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • to taste sea salt
  • to taste black pepper

Preparation

1. Set oven to 375°F.

2. Slice of sweet potato using a mandolin or sharp knife and separately chop up tomatoes, onions and mushrooms.

3. Mix cottage cheese with egg white and set aside.

4. Sauté red onions with garlic paste, then add meat, tomatoes, cilantro, mushrooms, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning.

5. Build lasagna. Add sweet potato slices, meat sauce, cottage cheese and then repeat. Top with mozzarella.

6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 45 minutes at 375°F.
