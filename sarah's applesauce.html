Sarahs Applesauce

Source: http://allrecipes.com/Recipe/Sarahs-Applesauce/Detail.aspx?evt19=1

Rating: 5.0

Servings: 4

Description

"This applesauce is delicious! I make it for my younger brother all the time and he loves it - and he hates canned applesauce!"" - Author

Ingredients

  • 4 apples - peeled (cored and chopped)
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Preparation

In a saucepan, combine apples, water, sugar, and cinnamon. Cover, and cook over medium heat for 15 to 20 minutes, or until apples are soft. Allow to cool, then mash with a fork or potato masher.
