Teriyaki Meatball Stir Fry

Source: https://www.aidells.com/recipes/dinner/teriyaki-meatball-stir-fry

Rating: 0.0

Description

As an added bonus, serve over rice or udon noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice wine vinegar or white wine vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 package Aidells Chicken Teriyaki Meatballs
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 pound vegetables (sliced)
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch mixed with 2 teaspoons water
  • 3 cups brown rice

Preparation

1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, vinegar, sugar and ginger set aside.

2. Place the oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat and add meatballs. Sauté for 3 minutes. Stir in the onion and garlic and sauté 2 minutes. Add the vegetables, pour in the soy sauce mixture and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover and simmer 3-5 minutes until vegetables are tender. Stir in the cornstarch mixture and bring the sauce back to a boil for 1-2 minutes to thicken the sauce. Serve over rice or noodles.
