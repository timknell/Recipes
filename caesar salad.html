Caesar Salad

Rating: 4.0

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 5 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 3/4 teaspoon anchovy fillets (minced)
  • 2 romaine lettuce (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup Parmigiano cheese (finely grated)
  • 1 pack croutons

Preparation

Whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, vinegar, Worcestershire, salt, and anchovy in a bowl. Add lettuce and toss, sprinkle with cheese, salt and pepper to taste and toss well. Add croutons and serve.
